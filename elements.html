Damorquis

Daniel Morkisch
Germany

As a huge Witcher series fan Damorquis was with great anticipation looking forward to Gwent. In no time, he dropped Hearthstone for Gwent and didn't look back ever since. The way skill is rewarded and mistakes are punished in the game, that’s what makes Gwent so attractive. Currently he is managing Team Aretuza with Hennotje forming a hopefully huge part of the Gwent community. In real life he works as a behavioural economist. Game and decision theory skills give him the edge to consistently score top 100 over several seasons.
