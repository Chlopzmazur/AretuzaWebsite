Welcome!

Team Aretuza is an international competitive esports team consisting of passionate players who want to push the boundaries of GWENT!

Team Aretuza started as a playtesting group for GWENT in summer 2017. Its initial goal was to bring together the most competitive core of GWENTâ€™s playerbase to prepare for tournaments, play scrims and share strategies amongst themselves. Although the team has undergone several changes in its playerbase and structure, the idea stayed the same. Strive to be the best!

These days Team Aretuza has already established a stable playerbase consisting of several high ranking ProLadder players, who have made their appearance on several tournaments reveered by community, streamers, as well as talented deckbuilders, tournaments players and high ranking ranked players.

The future of the team look very promising. There are still positions which need to be filled, players who would fit right into friendly and competitive enviroment and event waiting to unfold!
